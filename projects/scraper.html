"How might we assist clients anonymize their online presence even if they don't know what information exists about themselves online?"

Objectives

  1. Quickly identify individuals online whose personal information matches those provided by clients
  2. Store all valid links with personally identifying information for client's consideration
  3. Create visual displays of the qualitative results on each page to summarize found identity

Scraper Demo Video

The recording below demonstrates the combing process undertaken by the developed scraper, as well as the qualitative results generated by each scrape. After entering personally identifying information provided by the client into their respective prompts, the application will perform a search using the specified queries and search engine. The URL results of each search are collected in a summative document (see Figure 1) and each URL is subsequently combed and their corpuses are analyzed in a term-frequency inverse-document frequency (TF-IDF) analysis.
